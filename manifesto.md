Cyphr: A Manifesto.

You are a sovereign being made in the image of God; your voice is a divine
endowment.

For too long you have been repressed by those who distort and suppress your
voice, obscuring Truth by encumbering your voice.

Claim your sovereign voice again.

Your voice contributes to the whole's understanding of Truth in a way only you
can contribute. Your value is immeasurable.

We need you.

Cyphr is a sword in service to you, honoring your sovereign voice,
acknowledging that your identity is yours.

With this sword, be empowered again,

The Cyphr authors
